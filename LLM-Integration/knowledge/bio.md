# Biography

Rayyan Moosani is a Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo and a President’s Scholarship recipient. He is passionate about problem-solving, innovation, and building technology that addresses global sustainability and societal challenges.

Raised in the United Arab Emirates, Rayyan was inspired by the country's rapid technological advancement and digital transformation. This environment shaped his interest in artificial intelligence, automation, and cybersecurity, leading him to pursue a career at the intersection of creative problem-solving and strategic engineering.

He focuses on building intelligent systems that improve education, streamline workflows, and enhance operational efficiency across industries.
